About Intramuros

The Walled City of Manila is best explored in the early morning or late afternoon. Wear breathable clothing, carry water, and respect heritage sites by following posted visitor guidelines.

  • Best time to visit: November to February for cooler walks.
  • Safety: Stay in well-lit routes and keep emergency numbers available.
  • Respectful tourism: Avoid littering and observe quiet zones in churches and memorial spaces.
